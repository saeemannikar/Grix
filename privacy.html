Privacy

Privacy for operational memory.

Grix helps teams keep track of decisions, owners, blockers, and reversals. It is for organizational clarity, not employee surveillance, and is designed around workspace control, clear boundaries, and minimal data use.

Trust boundary Grix only analyzes channels it is invited to.

Teams choose where Grix participates. Workspace owners can request deletion at any time.

What Grix processes

Grix processes workspace content only where it has been intentionally added or invited. That content may include messages, channel names, timestamps, user display names, and related context needed to create operational memory for the workspace.

Grix only analyzes channels it is invited to.

How data is used

Grix uses workspace data to provide the service: identifying decisions, tasks, blockers, approvals, reversals, summaries, and other team-alignment context. We may also use limited operational data to secure, debug, support, and improve the reliability of Grix.

Workspace data is used for your workspace experience. It is not designed for productivity scoring or individual performance review.

What we do not do

  • Grix does not sell customer data.
  • Grix does not use customer data to train public AI models.
  • Grix does not build employee productivity scores or individual performance profiles.
  • Grix does not analyze channels unless it has been invited into them.

Security basics

Communication with Grix is encrypted over HTTPS. Workspace data is isolated so one workspace's information is not exposed to another workspace.

Access to Grix should be limited to authorized workspace members and administrators according to your internal policies.

Control and deletion

Workspace owners can request deletion of workspace data at any time by contacting Grix. We may need to verify that the requester is authorized to act for the workspace before deletion is completed.

Revoking Grix's Slack access can stop future processing for a workspace, subject to Slack permissions and your workspace configuration.

Contact

For privacy questions, deletion requests, or workspace data requests, email hello@grix.so.
